Enter company slugs separated by commas. A slug is the company identifier used in that platform's job URL.

Find a slug in the company's Greenhouse URL
Find a slug at api.lever.co/v0/postings/<slug>

What happens when you submit?

  1. A GitHub Actions job starts within seconds
  2. It searches Greenhouse, Lever, and RemoteOK for your role
  3. Jobs are ranked by fit score, salary estimate, and seniority
  4. Results land in the Google Sheet below (usually within 3–5 minutes)
